=== RETURN POLICY ===

--- Returns ---
- Returns are accepted within 14 days of receiving your order.
- Items must be unused, in the same condition as received, and in the original packaging.
- Contact the store immediately if you need to return an item or an entire order.
- (Customer Support FAQ specifies refunds are available only for new and unused devices
   within 14 business days of delivery from mrfog.online.)

--- How to Initiate a Return ---
1. Email customer service at support@mrfog.online
2. Provide your order number and a brief explanation of the reason for the return.
3. Once the request is approved, the store sends detailed return shipping instructions.

--- Refunds ---
Once the returned item is received and inspected, the customer is notified about refund
approval. If approved, the refund is processed to the original payment method within a
reasonable timeframe.

--- Shipping Costs on Returns ---
Return shipping costs are the customer's responsibility, EXCEPT when the item is defective
or incorrect (in which case MR FOG covers it).

--- Related Refund Situations (from Terms & Customer Support) ---
- If a package is returned by USPS due to an incorrect address, a refund is issued minus
  the shipping fee.
- Orders may be canceled and refunded when: the shipping address is not valid, the order is
  a duplicate, or the buyer shows suspicious purchase behavior.
- Products purchased from a retail store (not mrfog.online) are subject to that retailer's
  return policy, not MR FOG's.

--- Order Issues ---
For any issue related to an order, contact the store via the customer service tab within
3 days of receiving the order, describing the issue in detail and including pictures, the
item code, and the product pack when needed.
