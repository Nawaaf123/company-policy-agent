=== PRIVACY POLICY (SUMMARY) ===

OVERVIEW
This policy describes how MR FOG Online Store collects, uses, and discloses information when a
person uses the service, and describes privacy rights. Personal data is used to provide and
improve the service. By using the service, the user consents to the collection and use of
information per this policy.

KEY DEFINITIONS
- Company: MR FOG Online Store.
- Country: Alberta, Canada.
- Website / Service: MR FOG Online Store, https://mrfog.online.
- Personal Data: information relating to an identified/identifiable individual.
- Usage Data: data collected automatically from use of the service or its infrastructure.
- Service Provider: third parties that process data on the company's behalf.

TYPES OF DATA COLLECTED
Personal Data may include: email address; first and last name; phone number; address, state,
province, ZIP/postal code, city.
Usage Data (collected automatically) may include: IP address, browser type/version, pages
visited, time/date and duration of visits, unique device identifiers, and diagnostic data.
On mobile devices it may include device type, device ID, IP, OS, and mobile browser type.

TRACKING TECHNOLOGIES & COOKIES
Uses cookies, web beacons, tags, and scripts. Cookie categories:
- Necessary/Essential (session cookies): authenticate users, prevent fraud, enable core
  services.
- Cookies Notice Acceptance (persistent): record whether users accepted cookie use.
- Functionality (persistent): remember choices such as login details or language preference.
Users can instruct their browser to refuse cookies, but some features may then be unavailable.

USE OF PERSONAL DATA
To provide/maintain and monitor the service; manage the user's account; perform the purchase
contract; contact the user (email, calls, SMS, push notifications); provide news, special
offers, and general marketing (unless opted out); manage user requests; handle business
transfers (mergers/acquisitions); and for analysis, usage trends, campaign effectiveness, and
service improvement.

RETENTION
Personal data is retained only as long as necessary for the stated purposes and to comply with
legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Usage data is generally retained
for a shorter period unless needed for security/functionality or legally required.

TRANSFER OF DATA
Data may be processed at the company's operating offices and other locations, potentially
outside the user's jurisdiction. Submitting information represents agreement to that transfer.
The company states it takes reasonable steps to keep data secure.

DISCLOSURE
Data may be disclosed in business transactions (merger/acquisition/asset sale), for law
enforcement, or when legally required or necessary to comply with legal obligations, protect
the company's rights/property, investigate wrongdoing, protect user/public safety, or protect
against legal liability.

SECURITY
The company uses commercially acceptable means to protect data but cannot guarantee absolute
security (no internet transmission or electronic storage is 100% secure).

CHILDREN'S PRIVACY
The service does not address anyone under 21 and does not knowingly collect personal data from
anyone under 21. If such data is discovered, the company takes steps to remove it.

LINKS TO OTHER WEBSITES
The service may link to third-party sites the company does not control; users should review
those sites' privacy policies.

CHANGES
The policy may be updated; changes are posted on the page with an updated "Last updated" date
and may be communicated via email or a prominent notice.
