=== SHIPPING POLICY ===

- Carrier: All orders ship via USPS.
- Delivery time: Allow 2-7 business days for delivery.
- Coverage: Ships to residential and business addresses in the United States only.
  (Note: the Terms & Conditions page also references Canada and the USA; the dedicated
   Shipping page states United States only. Treat the Shipping page as the specific policy.)
- Free shipping: Free standard shipping on all orders over $69 USD (after discounts).

--- Restricted / Invalid Shipping Addresses ---
MR FOG does NOT ship to:
- Military bases
- P.O. Boxes
- Government-restricted or high-security areas
- Any address outside of the United States

Orders placed with an invalid shipping address will be canceled and refunded automatically
without prior notice.

--- Processing / Ship Times (from Customer Support & Terms) ---
- Ships Monday through Friday.
- Orders placed before 2:00 PM CT (Mon-Thu) may ship the same day.
- Orders placed after the daily cutoff will ship the next business day.
- Order status is "Processing" for up to 24 hours before it is ready to ship.
- Friday afternoon and weekend orders are fulfilled starting the next business day.
- Shipping times are subject to product availability; delivery estimates come from the
  carrier and may be affected by weather, holidays, and destination.
- All domestic shipments are insured; shipping charges include insurance.
