=== CUSTOMER SUPPORT FAQ ===

Q1. How can I cancel my order?
Once an order is submitted and payment is processed, it cannot be canceled after tracking
information has been generated. To cancel, contact customer support immediately. Allow up to
24 business hours for the request to be processed.

Q2. How can I track my order?
After an order ships, the customer receives an email with tracking details. If not received,
allow 24 business hours before contacting support. Tracking is also available on the
"My Account" page (order status and tracking number). For delivery updates, use the USPS
website (https://tools.usps.com/) or contact USPS directly.

Q3. When will I receive my order?
- Standard carrier: USPS. Standard shipping typically takes 2-7 business days.
- Free standard shipping on all orders over $69 USD (after discounts).
- Ships Monday through Friday.
- Orders placed before 2:00 PM CT (Mon-Thu) may ship the same day.
- Orders placed after the cutoff ship the next business day.
- Shipping times depend on product availability; delivery estimates come from the carrier
  and may be affected by weather, holidays, and destination.

Q4. Do I need to pay any additional fees?
No fees beyond the order total shown at checkout are charged by MR FOG. However, payments are
processed through a third-party partner based in Hong Kong, so a customer's bank or card
issuer may apply a foreign transaction fee (international service / cross-border fee). That
fee is set and collected solely by the customer's financial institution.

Q5. Can I return my product for a refund?
Refunds are available only for new and unused devices within 14 business days of delivery
from mrfog.online. Contact customer support with the order number to begin a return.

Q6. What if I purchased my product from another store?
Products bought from a retail store follow that retailer's return policy. If the device is
believed to be defective, contact the store where it was purchased.

Q7. How can I get a wholesale account?
MR FOG has a wholesale division for retailers and distributors. Fill out the Distributor Form
on the website; a sales representative will make contact to discuss pricing, shipping, and
account setup.

Q8. Incomplete or incorrect address submissions
Customers should double-check the shipping address. Incorrect addresses can delay delivery or
cause returns. If a package is returned by USPS due to an incorrect address, a refund is
issued minus the shipping fee.

Q9. Are there any shipping limitations?
USPS cannot deliver to: government-restricted areas, military bases, and P.O. Boxes.

Q10. Shipping charge estimates
Shipping charges depend on: 24-hour processing time, package size, destination, special
instructions, residential vs. business address, and hazardous materials (batteries).
Shipping charges include insurance; all domestic shipments are insured. MR FOG takes
responsibility for orders shipped using its shipping accounts. For insurance inquiries,
contact customer service.

--- Support Hours & Contact ---
- Email: support@mrfog.online
- Support hours: Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM (EST).
- Typical response time: 1-2 business days (longer during peak seasons/promotions).
