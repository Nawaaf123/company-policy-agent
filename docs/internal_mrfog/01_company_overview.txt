=== ABOUT MR FOG ONLINE STORE ===

MR FOG Online Store is an online retailer of premium vaping devices, e-liquids, and
accessories. The store's stated focus is providing adult smokers with premium vaping
devices and innovative alternatives to traditional smoking.

--- Our Story ---
Established in 2018, MR FOG Online Store began with the vision of providing adult smokers
worldwide with premium vaping devices and innovative alternatives to traditional smoking.
It started as a small team of product developers and grew into an international brand.
Over roughly five years the company built a customer community across Canada, the United
States, and beyond. Product range has evolved from early single-device launches to
multi-feature vape systems, with an emphasis on style, performance, and safety.

--- Our Mission ---
The mission is to support adult smokers seeking a safer and more enjoyable alternative to
traditional cigarettes, offering a range of high-performance vape devices that allow
personal customization while maintaining safety standards. The company emphasizes customer
education (product guides, usage tips, transition information) and states that while vaping
can reduce certain risks compared to combustible tobacco, it is not risk-free. The company
states it operates under governmental regulations and applies atomization technology,
durable materials, and quality control.
