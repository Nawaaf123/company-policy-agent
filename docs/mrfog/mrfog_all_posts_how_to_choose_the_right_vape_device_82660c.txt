Frequently Asked Questions
Which MR FOG device is best for beginners?
Many adult users who are new to vaping choose disposable devices because they require no setup or maintenance. Products like MR FOG NOVA and MR FOG AURA offer ready-to-use convenience with long-lasting performance.
What is the difference between MR FOG NOVA and MR FOG AURA?
Both are premium disposable vapes, but they focus on different experiences. NOVA offers up to 36,000 puffs with dual-core heating and a TFT display, while AURA delivers up to 60,000 puffs, dual-mesh heating, adjustable airflow, and the new Splash flavor collection.
Why choose the MR FOG Switch Pod Kit instead of a disposable vape?
The Switch Pod Kit combines a rechargeable battery with replaceable prefilled pods. This allows users to change flavors without replacing the entire device while still enjoying the convenience of a prefilled system.
Is the MR FOG DRT suitable for experienced vapers?
Yes. The DRT open-system platform is designed for adult users who prefer refillable devices and greater customization over their vaping experience.
Which MR FOG vape device lasts the longest?
The MR FOG AURA currently offers the highest puff capacity in the lineup, delivering up to 60,000 puffs , while NOVA provides up to 36,000 puffs , making both excellent choices for users seeking extended performance.
Can I switch flavors without buying a new device?
Yes. The MR FOG Switch Pod Kit allows users to replace only the pod while continuing to use the same rechargeable battery module. This makes changing flavors simple and convenient.
What features make MR FOG devices different from traditional disposable vapes?
Many MR FOG products include advanced technologies such as TFT displays, adjustable power modes, mesh or dual-mesh heating systems, fast Type-C charging, airflow adjustment, and high-capacity batteries that go beyond the features found in many standard disposable devices.
Does MR FOG offer different types of vape devices?
Yes. The MR FOG lineup includes high-capacity disposable vapes, rechargeable pod systems with replaceable pods, refillable open-system devices, and bottled e-liquids, allowing adult users to choose the device that best matches their vaping preferences.
