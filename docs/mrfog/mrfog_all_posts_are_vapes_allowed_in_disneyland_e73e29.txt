Are Vapes Allowed in Disneyland? A Complete Visitor Guide
If you’re planning a trip to Disneyland, you might be wondering:
👉 are vapes allowed in Disneyland?
are vapes allowed in Disneyland?
The answer is simple—but the details matter.
👉 No, vaping is not allowed inside Disneyland parks. 👉 However, guests can bring vape devices and use them only in designated areas outside the park.
No, vaping is not allowed inside Disneyland parks.
Understanding how Disney enforces this rule can help you avoid interruptions during your visit.
Disneyland’s Vaping Policy Explained
Disneyland’s Vaping Policy : Disney made a major change in May 2019 , removing all smoking and vaping areas from inside its parks.
Disneyland’s Vaping Policy :
May 2019
This policy applies to:
Disneyland Park
Disney California Adventure
Walt Disney World (Florida)
Most Disney-owned public areas
Electronic cigarettes are treated the same as traditional cigarettes.
Where Vaping Is NOT Allowed
You cannot vape anywhere inside Disneyland, including:
Ride queues and attractions
Restaurants and cafés
Outdoor walkways
Restrooms
Hotel common areas
Parking structures
Even outdoor areas inside the park are vape-free.
Can You Bring a Vape Into Disneyland?
Yes.
👉 You are allowed to bring a vape into Disneyland.
This includes:
Disposable vapes
Pod systems
Vape pens
Small devices in bags or pockets
However:
👉 You cannot use them inside the park
You cannot use them inside the park
What Happens at Security?
All bags are checked at the entrance.
Vapes are generally:
Allowed through security
Treated as personal electronic items
Security is focused on safety—not nicotine content.
Where Can You Vape at Disneyland?
If you want to vape, you must leave the park.
Designated Smoking Areas Are Located:
Outside park entrances
Near the Esplanade
Outside security screening zones
Limited areas in Downtown Disney
Important Note
👉 You must exit the park to vape 👉 Re-entry requires going through security again
This can take time, especially during busy hours.
What Happens If You Vape Inside Disneyland?
Disney enforces its rules strictly.
If you vape inside the park, you may:
Receive a warning from a cast member
Be asked to stop immediately
Be escorted out for repeated violations
In serious or repeated cases:
👉 You could be removed from the park for the day
Why Disneyland Banned Vaping?
Disney’s decision reflects broader trends in public spaces.
1. Family-Friendly Environment
Disney parks are designed for all ages, especially children.
Removing vaping reduces exposure to:
Nicotine vapor
Odors
Secondhand aerosol
2. Guest Experience
Smoke-free environments improve:
Air quality
Comfort for guests
Overall park atmosphere
3. Public Health Alignment
Many venues now follow similar rules:
Airports
Stadiums
Theme parks
Public transport hubs
Disney’s policy aligns with these trends.
Does This Rule Apply to Disney World?
Walt Disney World in Florida follows the same policy:
👉 No vaping inside parks 👉 Designated smoking areas outside
Tips for Vapers Visiting Disneyland
Plan Ahead
You’ll need to leave the park if you want to vape.
Use Compact Devices
Small devices are easier to carry and store.
Check Locations
Smoking areas may change—ask staff or check maps.
Avoid “Stealth Vaping”
Even discreet vaping can lead to:
Warnings
Staff intervention
Common Misunderstandings
“It’s okay outdoors inside the park”
❌ False — all areas inside are vape-free
“I can vape if no one sees me”
❌ Not recommended — enforcement is active
“Disposable vapes are allowed everywhere”
❌ You can bring them, but not use them inside
Final Answer
So, are vapes allowed in Disneyland?
👉 You can bring them 👉 You cannot use them inside the park
To vape, you must exit and use designated smoking areas outside.
Following these rules ensures a smoother, stress-free Disneyland experience.
Frequently Asked Questions
1. Can you vape inside Disneyland?
No. Vaping is prohibited throughout Disneyland parks.
2. Are disposable vapes allowed in Disneyland?
Yes, but they cannot be used inside the park.
3. Where can you vape at Disneyland?
Only in designated smoking areas outside park entrances.
4. Can you vape in Downtown Disney?
Only in limited designated areas, not in main public spaces.
5. What happens if you vape inside Disneyland?
You may receive a warning or be removed if you continue.
