Dual-Resistance Technology, Two Resistances In One Pod
DRT is the world’s first open-system device with Dual-Resistance Technology, offering two resistance options in a single pod. Easily switch between a tighter or smoother vape without changing pods, giving you greater control over flavor and vapor. Note: Two-pod options are available—0.4Ω/0.8Ω and 0.6Ω/1.2Ω.
