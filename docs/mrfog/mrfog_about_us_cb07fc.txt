Brand Values of MR FOG Team
Founded in 2018, MR FOG has always been focusing on user experience.
MR FOG offers innovative e-cigarettes globally, prioritizing quality and innovation.
Launched Elite, MAX, MAX PRO, MAX AIR, SWITCH, NOVA, SWITCH POD and other series, sold globally, loved by millions.
MR FOG has become one of the best-selling and most popular brands in the market.
MR FOG’S HISTORY
2018
The founding team formalized the MR FOG brand, and launched their first product in the same year.
2019
Launched MAX series in America, the first disposable e-cigarettes that could last over 1000 puffs.
2020
Launched MAX PRO series in America, the first e-cigarettes that could regulate air.
2021
Founded a subsidiary in Canada and launched the MAX AIR series, which sold over 40 million units a year.
2022
Launched SW5500 in North America, marking a major milestone for MR FOG.
2023
Launched SWITCH 15000, a customer-favorite disposable known for performance and flavor.
2024
Launched NOVA, a highly popular disposable series with expanded flavor collections.
2025
Launched SWITCH POD, MR FOG’s first clear tank pod system focused on quality and innovation.
2026
We would like to create a more brilliant future with you!
The Birth of MR FOG
“2018, Vape Lovers, International Brand”
In 2018, e-cigarette lovers from China, USA, and Canada united despite distance. Through in-depth communication, they founded MR FOG, blending ideas from their diverse backgrounds into one international brand.
Market-Driven, User-Involved R&D
“Research, Users, Regional Advantage”
MR FOG prioritizes market-driven product development, notably in North America. User engagement is central, supported by research teams in Canada and the US. Manufacturing in Shenzhen ensures safe, personalized e-cigarette products for global consumers.
Rise to the Top of the Market
“Quality, Competition, Top-Brand”
MR FOG excels in product design, flavor, and quality control, gaining a loyal global following. Its Elite, MAX, MAX PRO, MAX AIR, and SWITCH lines enjoy acclaim in 50+ countries, especially in North America, cementing its status as a top brand.
User First, Innovation Leads to the Future
“Innovation, Challenge, Industry Health”
MR FOG will persist with user-centric principles, prioritize user experience, tackle industry challenges, innovate products, and uphold its leading role in global vaping to foster industry health.
