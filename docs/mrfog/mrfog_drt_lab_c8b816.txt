Lab & Milestones
In 2022, MR FOG established a state-of-the-art lab, marking a key milestone in its commitment to innovation. With heavy investment in research and equipment, the lab became the foundation for developing and refining DRT technology. It serves as a hub for testing and optimizing products, ensuring they exceed industry standards .
DRT Milestones
2018
Atomization and flavor tuning technology
MR FOG is committed to enhancing the vaping experience through technological innovation, achieving breakthroughs in atomization performance and flavor consistency.
2019-2020
Atomization stability and battery management technology
MR FOG has significantly enhanced atomization stability through refined atomizer design, making key breakthroughs in battery management and intelligent atomization systems.
2021
Airflow Adjustment and Draw Resistance Control Technology
MR FOG has made significant breakthroughs in airflow and draw resistance design through advanced airway engineering, enhancing the smoothness of the draw and atomization stability of the product.
2022-2024
Adjustable Resistance Technology
While continuously optimizing flavor and atomization performance, MR FOG has made significant breakthroughs in adjustable resistance technology, which plays a crucial role in the future development of the DRT technology platform.
2024.12
DRT (Dual Resistance Technology)
In December 2024, MR FOG launched the revolutionary DRT technology, integrating two different resistance values within a single pod. This breakthrough enhances vapor stability, richness, and extends device lifespan.
DRT’s First-of-its-Kind Technology
DRT is the first in the industry to introduce “adjustable-resistance pod” technology, enabling adjustable resistance within a single pod.
E-Liquid Mixing Equipment
DRT uses advanced mixing equipment to ensure consistent and stable atomization, enhancing flavor and vapor production.
Battery Tester
DRT employs advanced battery testers to optimize battery components, improving stability and extending battery life.
