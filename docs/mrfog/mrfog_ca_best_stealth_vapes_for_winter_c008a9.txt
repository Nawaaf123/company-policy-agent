Consequently, if you accidentally leave your device inside a freezing cold car overnight, you will likely wake up to a completely dead battery. To successfully prevent this highly frustrating scenario, you must absolutely always keep your premium Mr Fog device stored securely in a warm interior clothing pocket close to your natural body heat.
