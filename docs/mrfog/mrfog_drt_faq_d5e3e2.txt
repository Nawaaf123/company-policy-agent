1.What is DRT Technology?
MR FOG DRT open system (Dual Resistance Technology) is an innovative vaping technology that integrates two different resistance coils within a single pod or atomizer. This design enhances vapor production, improves flavor consistency, and increases overall efficiency, providing a superior vaping experience.
2.How does DRT Technology improve flavor quality?
DRT open systems technologies uses dual resistance coils to optimize heat distribution, which results in smoother, more consistent vapor. This ensures richer, more intense flavors throughout the entire vaping session, from the first puff to the last.
3.Why is DRT Technology more efficient than single coil designs?
By combining two different resistances in one pod, DRT Technology ensures a more even heat distribution, reducing the risk of overheating and enhancing the overall vaporization process. This allows for better flavor delivery and more efficient use of e-liquid, which can also prolong the lifespan of the atomizer.
4.Does DRT Technology reduce e-liquid consumption?
Yes, DRT open systems technologies help to reduce e-liquid consumption by improving vaporization efficiency. The dual coils work in tandem to heat the e-liquid more evenly, reducing waste and ensuring that the e-liquid is used effectively.
5.How does DRT Technology extend the lifespan of my device?
DRT Technology’s dual resistance coils minimize the chances of overheating and coil burnout, which are common issues with single coil designs. The improved heat distribution helps reduce wear and tear, extending the lifespan of both the atomizer and the device itself.
6.What makes DRT Technology different from other vaping technologies?
Unlike conventional single coil designs, DRT Technology’s dual resistance coils provide a more efficient, balanced, and reliable vaping experience. It improves flavor consistency, reduces power consumption, and extends the life of the atomizer while delivering a superior vaping performance.
7.How does DRT Technology help with leak prevention?
DRT Technology incorporates advanced sealing and design features to minimize the risk of e-liquid leakage. The system ensures that e-liquid stays locked in the pod, maintaining efficiency and reducing the chances of leaks during use.
8.Is DRT Technology compatible with all vaping devices?
Currently, DRT open systems technologies are designed for specific MR FOG products equipped with dual resistance pods or atomizers. However, MR FOG plans to launch vaping devices that are compatible with DRT Technology, expanding its usability across a wider range of devices.
What is the MR FOG DRT device?
DRT open system (Dual Resistance Technology) is an innovative vaping technology that integrates two different resistance coils within a single pod or atomizer. This design enhances vapor production, improves flavor consistency, and increases overall efficiency, providing a superior vaping experience.The DRT open system device is the first open system product to use DRT technology.
What is the best way to store the DRT device?
“Please do not store the DRT device in a depleted state for more than two weeks. Before storage, it is recommended to fully charge the vaporizer and remove the pod. As with all nicotine-containing products, keep the DRT device out of reach of children and pets.”
Can I take the DRT device on a plane?
Devices without pre-filled e-liquid are typically allowed on airplanes. However, we recommend consulting the airline’s policy.
How long can the DRT device last on a single charge?
With a fully charged device, it can typically last about three days, depending on your usage.
Can I overcharge the battery?
The DRT device uses smart charging technology to reduce the risk of overcharging. However, we still recommend stopping the charge once fully charged.
How many puffs can I take with a fully charged DRT device?
The number of puffs depends on your vaping style and usage pattern.
Do DRT pods expire?
Generally, pods should be used as soon as possible after purchase. While DRT pods do not expire, their flavor and quality may decline after one year. If not used immediately, it is best to seal the pods in a vacuum-sealed package and store them in a dry environment at room temperature.
How should I dispose of a used DRT device?
The DRT device is not a disposable product and should be treated as an electronic device. Please follow local, regional, national, and/or international regulations for proper disposal at designated hazardous or special waste collection points.
Why isn't my DRT device producing vapor?
You can use a cotton swab dampened with alcohol (make sure to remove excess alcohol) to clean the contacts inside the DRT open system device and on the DRT pods. This can help resolve issues with poor contact between the pod and the vaporizer rod caused by moisture affecting the contacts.
Hold the mouthpiece facing upward, gently tap the DRT pod on a table, and then reinsert it into the vaporizer rod. This will help bring any trapped small air bubbles to the surface, ensuring that the e-liquid is fully absorbed by the coil.
If the issue persists, try using a different DRT pod to see if it improves.”
My DRT device is not charging properly.
Connect the charging port to a suitable power source. Use a dry cotton swab or a slightly damp one to clean the charging contacts on the vaporizer rod and charging base.”
The pod is leaking.
If the DRT pod leaks, remove it from the device, wipe off the leaked e-liquid, and reinsert it into the vaporizer rod. Minor leaks are normal, but DRT pods are of high quality, and the likelihood of leakage during regular use is minimal.
To prevent leaks, follow these tips:
Inhale gently – vigorous inhalation may draw e-liquid into the mouthpiece. Avoid applying excessive pressure while inhaling – do not squeeze or bite the mouthpiece. Secure the mouthpiece by gently pinching the outer edge to ensure it fits properly without forcing it. If your DRT device continues to leak excessively after following these steps, please store the device and contact us.”
There is moisture inside the pod packaging.
Changes in humidity and high altitudes can result in moisture inside the DRT pod packaging.

What materials are used for the DRT device and pods?
The DRT device features an aluminum shell that houses a lithium-ion battery, circuit board, and pressure sensor, all separated from the vapor channel and e-liquid. DRT pods are typically made from heat-resistant, food-grade plastic, containing a stainless steel vapor channel, industrial-grade silicone core, and a nickel-chromium alloy heating coil.”
What type of battery is used in the device?
The DRT open system device uses a lithium-ion polymer battery. All portable electronic devices with lithium-ion batteries carry rare but potentially serious safety risks, especially in high or low-temperature conditions. To ensure personal and property safety, charge the DRT device only within the temperature range of 5°C to 45°C (41°F to 113°F)
Does the magnet used in the device affect other electronic devices?
When connected to a USB charging port, the magnet on the base securely holds the vaporizer rod in place. Many electronic devices with magnets may interfere with each other. When charging on a laptop, the DRT device may trigger the laptop’s internal magnet, causing it to enter sleep mode. This will not damage your computer, but if it occurs, try using a different USB port. Additionally, keep the DRT device away from key cards, credit cards, other magnetic strip items, and pacemakers by at least 15cm.”
