What is the MR FOG SWITCH POD KIT Splash Series?
The MR FOG SWITCH POD KIT Splash Series is a rechargeable pod system featuring a reusable battery module, replaceable prefilled pods, up to 45,000 puffs, and refreshing icy Splash flavors.
What makes the Splash Series different?
Each Splash flavor includes a signature icy slush finish that enhances smoothness, refreshment, and flavor clarity while maintaining the original flavor profile.
How many puffs does the SWITCH POD KIT provide?

Yes. Users can choose between ECO, BOOST, and TURBO modes, adjust airflow, and customize screen brightness.
